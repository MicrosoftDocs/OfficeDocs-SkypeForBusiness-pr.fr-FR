## <a name="making-the-best-use-of-the-ip-phone-inventory-report"></a><span data-ttu-id="d5e2c-129">Utilisation optimale du Rapport d’inventaire de téléphonie IP</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="d5e2c-129">Making the Best Use of the IP Phone Inventory Report</span></span>
